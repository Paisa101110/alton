About the Resort

What is
Alton Towers?

Alton Towers Resort is the UK's most iconic leisure and holiday complex — a world of extraordinary thrills, family memories and magical escapes nestled across over 500 acres of breathtaking Staffordshire countryside.

Home to more than 40 incredible attractions, the resort goes far beyond a traditional theme park. From heart-pounding roller coasters to relaxing waterpark experiences, themed hotels, and award-winning kids' areas — there is something here for every age and daring level.

Alton Towers is proudly part of Merlin Entertainments, the world's second-largest visitor attraction operator, ensuring world-class experiences across every visit.

Stay & Play

Where to Sleep

Stay on-site and wake up steps from the magic. Choose from unique themed hotels, cosy forest lodges, or starlit pods.

🏰

Themed Hotels

Three extraordinary themed hotels — including the exclusive CBeebies Land Hotel — offer immersive overnight experiences unlike anywhere else. Each hotel tells its own story from check-in to check-out.

🌌

Stargazing Pods

Spend the night under a canopy of stars in a private Stargazing Pod. These intimate, modern capsules are designed for unforgettable couples' getaways or unique family nights with a view of the cosmos.

🌲

Woodland Lodges

Nestled deep in the enchanted forest, the Woodland Lodges offer cosy, self-contained cabins for families seeking a nature-inspired retreat within the resort grounds. Pure magic at every turn.

Visitor Info

Plan Your Visit

  • 🕙
    Opening Hours Typical park hours: 10:00 am – 8:00 pm. Check the official site for seasonal variations.
  • 💳
    Cashless Resort Alton Towers operates as a fully cashless venue. Payment by card or mobile wallet only throughout the resort.
  • 🎟️
    Book in Advance Pre-booking your tickets online is strongly recommended to guarantee entry, especially during school holidays and peak weekends.
  • 🅿️
    Parking Available On-site parking available for day visitors. Parking is included free with all hotel and short break packages.

    Accessibility Alton Towers is committed to accessibility for all guests. Ride accessibility guides and assistance are available on-site.
